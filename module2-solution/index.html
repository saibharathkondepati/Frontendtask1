Cricket Players

KLRahul

Kannur Lokesh Rahul is an Indian international cricketer. He is the current vice-captain of the India national cricket team in One Day Internationals and Twenty20 Internationals. He plays for Karnataka in domestic cricket. He is the current captain of Lucknow Super Giants in the Indian Premier League

Yuvraj

Yuvraj Singh is a former Indian international cricketer who played in all formats of the game. He is an all-rounder who batted left-handed in the middle order and bowled slow left-arm orthodox, and is known for his aggressive batting style and all-round ability.

Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is a former international cricketer who captained the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. He is currently the captain of Chennai Super Kings, a franchise based team of Indian Premier League
